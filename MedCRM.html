Import from Salesforce

Follow these steps to export your contacts from Salesforce and import them here in one click.

1
In Salesforce, click the App Launcher (grid icon, top left) and open Contacts or Accounts.
2
Click the list view you want to export (e.g. "All Contacts"). Then click the gear icon ⚙️ → Export.
3
Choose Export as CSV. Make sure these fields are included: First Name Last Name Email Phone Account Name Title Mailing City Mailing State
4
Save the CSV file to your computer.
5
Come back here and click "Import CSV / Excel" in the left sidebar. MedCRM will auto-detect Salesforce column names and map them for you.
💡 Tip: MedCRM auto-recognizes standard Salesforce column names like "First Name", "Last Name", "Account Name", "Mobile", etc. — no manual mapping needed for standard exports.
